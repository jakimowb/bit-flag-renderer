Bit Flag Renderer - a QGIS Plugin to visualize bit flags in quality images. Earth observation data, like from the Landsat or Sentinel satellites, is often provided with bit-packed quality information, like confidence on cloud cover, cloud shadow or other information. The Bit Flag Renderer allows to visualize the state of selected bits or neighbored bit positions.
