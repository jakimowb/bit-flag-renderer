Visualization of bit flags in raster quality images.

Features

  • visualizes single bit flags (on/off) and groups of bits (2 bits = 4 states, 3 bits = 8 states)
  • layer legend shows flag colors
  • predefined flag schemes, e.g. for Landsat 4-8 quality bands
  • allows to specify, save and reload own flag schemes
  • Documentation https://bit-flag-renderer.readthedocs.io/en/latest/
  • Repository https://bitbucket.org/jakimowb/bit-flag-renderer

The Bit Flag Renderer is developed at Humboldt-Universität zu Berlin, Earth Observation Lab as part of the Land Use Monitoring System (LUMOS) project, funded by the Belgian Science Policy Office as part of the Stereo-III research program (grant no. SR/01/349).
